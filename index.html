#searchforendorphins

Explore an alternative jogging route with a friend this weekend!

East Coast Park

Leisurely run along the coastline with easy access and beautiful views.

Distance: 3.8 km (one way)
Terrain: Paved, coastal
Difficulty: Easy
Common Animals: Monitor Lizard, Crab-eating Macaque, Butterflies

MacRitchie Reservoir

Run along the scenic reservoir, surrounded by nature with plenty of wildlife.

Distance: 8 km (loop)
Terrain: Gravel, nature trails
Difficulty: Moderate
Common Animals: Long-tailed Macaque, Monitor Lizard, Butterflies

Bukit Timah Nature Reserve

For a more challenging run, explore Singapore's highest peak with lots of natural beauty and wildlife.

Distance: 11 km (loop)
Terrain: Hilly, nature trails
Difficulty: Hard
Common Animals: Long-tailed Macaque, Common Palm Civet, Kingfisher

Marina Bay & Gardens by the Bay

Supertrees! Take a scenic jog along Singapore's skyline.

Distance: 5-8 km (loop)
Terrain: Paved, smooth, flat
Difficulty: Easy
Common Animals: Kingfisher, Herons, Monkeys, Butterflies

Southern Ridges

Mix of forest pathways and scenic viewpoints. Occasional steep inclines, you'll get a workout.

Distance: 10km
Terrain: Paved, hilly, varied terrain
Difficulty: Moderate
Common Animals: Squirrels, Monkeys, Yellow-vented bulbul, Asian koels

Singapore River / Clarke Quay

Urban feels, along the river and through the city.

Distance: 5-6km
Terrain: Paved, urban, flat
Difficulty: Moderate
Common Animals: Occasional herons along the river banks!

Sentosa Island

Beachfront chills.

Distance: 5-6km
Terrain: Paved, beachfront, slight inclines
Difficulty: Moderate
Common Animals:Monkeys, Coastal birds, Occasional turtles by the shoreline

West Coast Park

Good shaded spots for a respite when you need to!

Distance: 5km
Terrain: Paved, gravel paths
Difficulty: Easy
Common Animals:Kingfishers, Herons, Mangrove Crabs, Monitor Lizards

Punggol Waterway Park

Family-friendly, ideal for a lesiurely jog. Great waterfront views and green spaces.

Distance: 5-7km
Terrain: Paved, flat
Difficulty: Moderate
Common Animals: Otters, Monitor Lizards, Egrets, Kingfishers

Singapore Botanic Gardens (Heritage Trail)

Mix of lush greenery, heritage features and a serene atmosphere.

Distance: 3km
Terrain: Paved, flat
Difficulty: Easy
Common Animals: Sunbirds, Swallows, Yellow-vented bulbuls, Squirrels, Monitor Lizards

Bishan-Ang Mo Kio Park

Mix of lush greenery, heritage features and a serene atmosphere.

Distance: 3-4km
Terrain: Paved, flat
Difficulty: Easy
Common Animals: Otters, Herons, Egrets, Kingfishers, Squirrels, Monitor Lizards

Bidadari Park

Good mix of urban and nature. Some boardwalks and gravel paths!

Distance: 2-3km
Terrain: Paved, flat
Difficulty: Easy
Common Animals: Oriental-pied hornbills, Whiskered tern, Yellow-vented bulbuls
